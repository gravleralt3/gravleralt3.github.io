What is an exam clash?
An examination clash is defined as multiple examinations that are scheduled on the same day at the same time, and that are listed on the UBC Exam Schedule for the current winter session.
 What do I do if I have an exam clash?
Visit our examination policy website for procedures for handling an exam clash.
 What is an exam hardship?
Exam hardship is defined in detail in the UBC Vancouver Calendar and UBC Okanagan Calendar. Please refer to these sources for more information.
 What do I need to bring to my exam?
The exam guidelines website provides an overview of what to bring and not to bring to an exam. Any additional material will be specified by your professor.
