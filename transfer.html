How is transfer credit for courses that I completed at another institution on a Letter of Permission (LOP) added to my UBC record?
To have courses added to your record, you will need to submit a Request and/or Review of Added Credit form, your LOP (Letter of Permission), and the official transcript from the other institution to UBC Enrolment Services.

 How do I find out the status of my Added Credit request?
Inquiries can be sent to academicrecord.support@ubc.ca.
 Will transfer credit be shown on my official UBC transcript?
Your transfer credit will not be printed on your official UBC transcript. You will need to provide transcripts from your other institution(s) when applying for future studies.
 Where can I inquire about specific transfer credit I received?
Questions regarding the articulation of credit and overall degree planning should be directed to your faculty's Academic Advising Office:
  • Vancouver
  • Okanagan

For more information, please visit the Transfer credit website.
 How long does it take to process my added credit request?
It takes at least 4 to 6 weeks of processing time before your academic record is updated. Processing time may increase during peak periods: April-May and October-November.
 Do I need a pre-approved Letter of Permission (LOP) from my faculty to transfer courses?
Yes. Without a pre-approved LOP, requests for transfer credit may be rejected.
